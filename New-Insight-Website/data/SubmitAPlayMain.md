### Submit A Play
Do you have a play that you would like to stage with Insight? We'd love to hear about it!
Please use the form here to submit the details of the play you would like to stage, and we will be in touch once we have reviewed the submission to discuss.
We take play submissions year-round, so please don't hesitate to submit based on upcoming shows - we are planning well in advance and would love to have your proposal to consider.
