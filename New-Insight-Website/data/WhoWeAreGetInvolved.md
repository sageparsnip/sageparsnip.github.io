### Want To Get Involved?
As an amateur group, our continuation demands new blood - it could be yours!
We are always eager to get new members into the Insight fold - whether you want to be under the lights or operating them, on the stage or building it, welcoming the audiences or entertaining them, every member is essential.

We hold Readings For Fun each July, as casual and fun get-together to read some scripts, which is an excellent way to get acquainted with the group. Otherwise, you can feel free to audition for our Spring or Autumn productions (no former experience required!), volunteer for Set Construction, Light and Sound, Backstage, Front of House, or more - if you're willing to help out, we can use your skills. 

Feel free to drop an email at the address at the bottom of the page, reach out to our Facebook or Instagram pages, or join our Whatsapp community to get the latest on what we're up to.
